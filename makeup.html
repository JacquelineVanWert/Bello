About this class

Color can make a statement and reveal your individuality. In this lesson we’ll discuss how color and style are key to self-expression and how undertones can help you find the right colors. Also, in this lesson we’ll clears up a misconception about nude makeup and deeper skintones.

Class Length: 17 video lessons (3 hrs 48 min) Category: Makeup fundamentals
