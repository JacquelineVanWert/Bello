Q: Can I use concealer as an eyeshadow primer?

A: Yes you can, but it is not recommended. Eyeshadow primers are designed to prolong the wear time of your eyeshadows and help them appear more vibrant.

Q: Is the sunscreen found in foundation enough protection?

A: No! While it has sunscreen it is not enough protection and it doesn't last long enough to replace a stand alone product.

Q: What skin tones work best with the yellow powder?

A: The secret is that yellow translucent powder blends beautifully with all skin tones and is a must-have for brightening and neutralizing.
